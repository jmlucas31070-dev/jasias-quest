# Astral Beasts — A Journey Across Nine Regions

*A new adventure for Jasia's Quest.*

---

## A console in your bedroom

Something new sits beside your wardrobe — a slim crystal **Astral
Console**. Sunny has one too, on her side table down the hall. Touch
it, and the world bends sideways into a bright, secret layer where
strange creatures roam: the **Astral Beasts**.

There are nine regions out there. Nine clades. Nine gyms, nine
champions, nine corners of a syndicate that calls itself **Team
Eclipse** and means to dim every sky in turn.

They want this world quieter.

You don't.

---

## What you'll do

* Begin in the **Emberlands**, where the Cinderkin smoulder in the
  basalt fields outside Ashbridge. The professor will hand you a folio
  — your first Beast Dex — and five **Spirit Orbs**.
* Find a wild beast. Throw an orb to bind it, or fight it the
  old-fashioned way. Caught beasts ride with you as **bound focus
  items** — equip one and it fights at your side, lending you its
  signature resonance.
* Earn each region's Crest from its gym leader. Then face the
  Champion. Their defeat opens the road north.
* Catch Team Eclipse in the act. Every region hides one of their
  hideouts; every Admin guards an evolution stone.
* Fill nine dexes. Earn the **Master Dex**. And then the league will
  call you back for the **Long Tournament** — every gym and every
  champion, harder than before, all in a single ladder.

## Catch, fight, evolve, breed

* **Catch** with Spirit Orbs — basic, lure, great, dusk, master. Higher
  tiers for higher beasts. A **trainer rank** climbs from 1 to 100 as
  you win, catch, and turn in dexes; some beasts won't be bindable
  until your rank is high enough.
* **Fight** to skip the orb and let your other beasts do the talking.
* **Shinies and variants** roam the wilds rarely — same beast, brighter
  spell, prouder bearing.
* **Evolve** at any Beast Center breeder using a clade stone or a
  Resonant Shard.
* **Breed** by burning a Pair Incense at the breeder. An egg comes
  home with you.

## Two travelers, one console

Both consoles open the same world. Switch between Jasia and Sunny —
the same Astral Beasts roam the same regions, the same dexes fill, the
same Crests you earn count for the household. Two trainers, one
journey, nine regions, ninety beasts to find — and three forms each,
if you keep your eyes open.

The console is humming.

Time to go.
