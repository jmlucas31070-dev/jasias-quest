# Jasias Quest — The Island Chronicles
## A Brand New Adventure Awaits!

Brave adventurer, the world of Jasias Quest has grown beyond anything anyone imagined.
A vast new land stretches before you — fourteen regions across the main island,
eight distant isles reachable by boat, and secrets hiding in every shadow.

## What's New?

### The Main Island
Starting at the **Harbor**, journey east through a living world: ancient forests where
wolves hunt in packs, treacherous swamps hiding dark secrets, a bustling town with
a market, an adventurer's guild, and a school — and a wild coastline ending at the
open sea. If you dare go further.

### Eight Offshore Islands

From the **Harbor**:
- **Crow Isle** — a rocky pirate stronghold. Will you reclaim it for honest folk?
- **Serpent Isle** — dense jungle paths, an ancient shaman, and the Great Serpent.
- **Ember Isle** — volcanic rivers and a legendary forge master in need of help.
- **Mist Isle** — perpetual fog, ghostly wraiths, and a cryptic oracle.

From the southern **Dock**:
- **Storm Isle** — weather-battered cliffs and ferocious thunder bears.
- **Coral Isle** — crystal-clear waters, nesting sea turtles, and rare pearl diving.
- **Ash Isle** — desolate volcanic ash fields where massive golems roam.
- **Phantom Isle** — ghost-haunted mists concealing the secrets of drowned sailors.

### Dungeons and Dangers
- Delve into the **Orc Caves** north of the slope and face Grak the Warchief.
- Navigate a **12-room Forest Maze** leading to a hidden lake — and the serpent within.
- Brave **24 dark mine tunnels** before climbing to a breathtaking mountain summit.
- Survive the **Swamp Maze** to confront the legendary Bog Horror.
- Exorcise the **Graveyard** of Ghost Lord Morden's relentless undead army.
- Descend **12 chambers** into the Dragon Cave and face Scovareth the Ancient.
- **Dive beneath the sea** — if you can find the right equipment — to battle a colossal Deep Octopus.

### A World Full of Life
- 47 monsters and animals across diverse ecosystems.
- 30 NPCs with their own stories and quests to offer.
- 48 quests to discover and complete across the entire expansion.
- A market, inns, pubs, a bank, a magic shop, a post office, and an adventurer's guild.
- A school full of curious students and dedicated teachers.
- 52 daily-reset foraging areas — herbs, berries, gems, and more waiting to be found.

### Technology Meets Adventure
Both Jasia and Sunny now have computers in their bedrooms. Use them to connect to
**Nemesis** — a legendary text-based world where a whole new kind of adventure begins.

### The Adventurer's Guild
Join the guild in town, complete twelve increasingly dangerous tasks, and earn the
legendary **Guildmaster's Blade** — the finest weapon the island has ever seen.

## Tips for New Adventurers
- Talk to every NPC — they often carry quests, hints, or items worth trading.
- Search the outdoors carefully — foraging spots reset every day.
- You will need a special item to explore the deepest part of the sea.
- Grak the Warchief, the Bog Horror, Ghost Lord Morden, Scovareth the Ancient,
  and the Deep Octopus are the five great challenges of this expansion. Face them last.
- Stock up on food and ale at inns and pubs before long dungeon runs.
