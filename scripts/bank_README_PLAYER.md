# BANK OF THE REALM — Player Guide

> *"Stop carrying a sack of coin into every dungeon. The Realm's tellers
> have you covered."*

The wandering days of stuffing every gold piece into your belt are **over**.
The Bank of the Realm has opened its doors across the kingdom, and your
character can finally do what every adventurer dreams of: **deposit**,
**withdraw**, **borrow**, and **stash treasure** in a real, persistent vault.

---

## Why you'll love it

- **One ledger, every city.** Drop 5,000 gold at the Westgate branch,
  walk to Ados, and pull it back out at that teller. Your bank notes work
  at every counter in the realm.
- **Bank notes in 7 denominations** — 10g, 50g, 100g, 500g, 1000g, 5000g,
  10000g. Light to carry. Easy to count.
- **The Lockbox.** Hand the teller your prized Heirloom Crown, your Cut
  Diamond, your Sealed Scroll. Walk away with a numbered voucher. Redeem
  it at any branch — the exact item is waiting for you.
- **Mystery Cache.** Feeling lucky? 50 gold buys you a sealed cache from
  the bank's overflow vault. The reward inside is rolled fresh on the
  spot — and it might be worth ten times what you paid.
- **Loans at 10%.** Need 1,000 gold *right now* for a sword you can't
  walk away from? Borrow it. Pay it back at any teller, in any city,
  whenever you next have the coin. (One loan at a time — pay it off
  before borrowing again.)

---

## How to use it

1. Walk into Westgate Market and look for the new bank stall on the
   south-east row.
2. Speak to the **Banker** behind the counter. The first time, ask to
   open an account — your free **Bank Membership Card** unlocks the rest
   of the menu.
3. From then on, every time you talk to any banker in any city you'll
   see five clear options:
   - *Deposit gold*
   - *Withdraw gold*
   - *Take or repay a loan*
   - *Use my lockbox*
   - *How does the bank work?*

---

## What's in the vault

- **16 storable valuables** — gems, scrolls, signet rings, heirloom blades
  and shields, embroidered cloaks, ancient relics, and more.
- **3 NPCs per branch** — banker, clerk, guard. Talk to the clerk for the
  rate sheet.
- **Branches in 5 cities** ready to drop in: Westgate, Ados, Crossglen,
  Fallhaven, Vilegard. (Westgate is wired up out of the box.)
- **A reusable bank template** so modders can drop a branch into any city
  in seconds.

---

## A word from the bank

> *"Member or no, every gold piece you trust to us is yours forever. The
> ledger remembers. The vault never forgets. Welcome to the Bank of the
> Realm."*

Open your account. Stop hauling sacks. Start adventuring lighter.
