# Jasias Quest — Grand Addition Pack
## A New Chapter Awaits, Adventurer!

---

> *"The island holds more secrets than the shore can show. Beyond the harbor, ten libraries stand silent and waiting. Five guilds offer power, prestige, and purpose. Two hundred souls walk these streets — some richer than they let on. The astral realm hums with ancient danger. And in the right hands, a wooden rod can become a weapon that makes dragons tremble."*

---

## What's New?

### 📚 Ten Libraries — Knowledge Is the Greatest Weapon

Scattered across the island, ten grand libraries have opened their doors. Each is staffed by a Scholar NPC who will teach you skills no fighter, thief, or mage can learn through combat alone:

- **Foraging** — Find food and herbs in the wild. Never go hungry again.
- **Mining** — Dig iron, copper, silver, and the legendary mithril from the earth.
- **Cooking** — Turn raw ingredients into hearty stews, pies, and dried meats.
- **Sewing** — Craft gloves, boots, cloaks, hats, and enchanted robes.
- **Gardening** — Plant seeds, tend crops, harvest wheat, carrots, and magical herbs.
- **Weapon Forging** — Hammer out iron swords, steel axes, and mithril blades.
- **Armor Forging** — Protect yourself with iron plate, steel armor, and mithril mail.

The Grand Libraries teach multiple skills and house merchants stocking rare materials. Seek them all out — the Scholar's Grand Tour quest awaits those who visit every one.

---

### ⚔️ Fighter's Guild — Strength Is Everything

Join the Fighter's Guild and rise through three ranks of combat mastery:

- **Rank 1 — Recruit**: Learn **Bash** — a powerful strike with a chance to stun enemies cold.
- **Rank 2 — Journeyman**: Unlock **Guild Forging** — forge weapons and armor of superior quality that surpasses normal limits.
- **Rank 3 — Master Fighter**: Master the **Second Strike** — attack twice every turn. The battlefield is yours.

Your Fighter's Guild skills stay with you **forever** — even if you later join another guild.

---

### 🗡️ Thieves' Guild — Fortune Favors the Swift

The Thieves' Guild is watching. Find them, and they'll watch your back:

- **Rank 1 — Cutpurse**: Gain the ability to **Use Magic Items** — potions, scrolls, and wands created by mages, clerics, and druids are yours to use.
- **Rank 2 — Shadow**: Learn to **Steal** — 200 ordinary townspeople walk these streets with full pockets. Help yourself.
- **Rank 3 — Master Thief**: Master the art of **Backstab** — strike before combat begins with devastating force. A clean kill means no fight at all — just loot.

Your Thief skills stay with you **forever** — even if you later join another guild.

---

### 🔮 Mage's Guild — Power Beyond the Sword

The Mage's Guild rewards those who study the arcane:

- **Rank 1 — Apprentice**: **Scribe Scrolls** — write magical scrolls at a writing desk, including Recall Scrolls to teleport home.
- **Rank 2 — Journeyman Mage**: **Brew Potions** — craft mana potions, strength tonics, and more at a heavy cauldron.
- **Rank 3 — Master Mage**: **Craft Wands and Staffs** — imbue rods with fire, lightning, and hexing magic. Build legendary weapons of pure arcane energy.

---

### ✨ Cleric's Guild — The Divine Hand Heals and Strikes

The Cleric's Guild serves a higher purpose — and you can too:

- **Rank 1 — Acolyte**: **Scribe Holy Scrolls** — write healing and holy scrolls. Craft a Recall Scroll to return to the guild in an instant.
- **Rank 2 — Initiate**: **Brew Holy Potions** — craft health potions, antidotes, and blessed tonics.
- **Rank 3 — High Cleric**: **Craft Holy Wands and Staffs** — channel divine light into powerful healing instruments.

---

### 🌿 Druid's Circle — Nature's Power Is Yours

The Druid's Circle draws power from root, rain, and stone:

- **Rank 1 — Seedling**: **Scribe Nature Scrolls** — write scrolls of healing and entanglement. Recall Scrolls call the grove to you.
- **Rank 2 — Grove Keeper**: **Brew Nature Potions** — regeneration brews, speed tonics, and wild draughts.
- **Rank 3 — Elder Druid**: **Craft Nature Wands and Staffs** — wield instruments of living wood crackling with druidic power.

---

### 🪄 Wands and Staffs — Spells in Your Hands

Every mage, cleric, and druid spell you can cast can now be bound into a wand or staff by a skilled crafter. Wands are light and reusable. Staffs deal physical damage AND channel magic. Even a Thief with Use Magic Items can wield them.

Wands available: **Fireball, Lightning, Healing, Hexing, Nature's Touch, Bog Curse, Inferno**

Staffs available: **Staff of Flame, Staff of the Storm, Holy Staff, Staff of the Grove, Staff of Hexing, Staff of the Inferno**

---

### 📜 Recall Scrolls — Never Be Far From Home

Four Recall Scrolls can be crafted or purchased — one for each spellcasting guild and one for the Adventurer's Guild. Use one and you'll be standing in your guild's hall before the ink is dry.

---

### 🏠 Home Upgrades — Live Like an Adventurer

Your home isn't just for sleeping anymore. Merchants now sell:

- **Personal Computer** — browse world lore and send messages. Available in 1-room homes and above.
- **Game Console** — play mini-games for fun. Available in 1-room homes and above.
- **Writing Desk** — scribe scrolls from the comfort of home. (4-room homes only)
- **Heavy Cauldron** — brew potions without leaving the house. (4-room homes only)
- **Wand Crafting Table** — craft wands at home. (4-room homes only)
- **Staff Crafting Table** — assemble staffs at home. (4-room homes only)

---

### 👥 Two Hundred Townspeople — Not All Pockets Are Empty

Two hundred ordinary citizens now walk the streets of the island. Merchants, farmers, scholars, soldiers, fishers, priests. They have names, occupations, and opinions about you.

They also have coin.

A skilled thief will find opportunity everywhere. Just don't get caught.

---

### 🌌 The Astral Realm — Leave Safely or Pay the Price

The Astral Beast World now operates by the same rules as the MUD. Find the **Astral Gate Keeper** to safely return to the mortal world. Close the game without saying goodbye, and the astral energies will have their toll.

---

## How to Start

- Visit the **harbor** and look for new doors — the libraries and guild halls await
- Speak to any **Scholar** to browse available skills and buy skill manuals
- Seek out **Guildmaster Brogan** at the Fighter's Guild to test your mettle
- Check the notice board at the **Adventurer's Guild** for new quests
- Explore. Ask questions. Everything is connected.
