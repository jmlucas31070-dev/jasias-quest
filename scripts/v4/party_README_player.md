# Jasias-Quest: The Companions Expansion

## Adventure Awaits -- And You Do Not Have to Face It Alone!

For too long, adventurers in Jasias-Quest have walked the perilous roads alone.
Bandits, monsters, ancient curses -- all faced by a single brave soul.

That changes now.

The Companions Expansion brings the most exciting update to Jasias-Quest:
a living, breathing party system that lets you recruit real adventurers,
build legends, and face challenges that were never meant to be conquered alone.

## Find Your Companions at the Pub

Across the land, 20 pubs have thrown open their doors to adventurers seeking comrades.
Walk into The Rusty Flagon, The Golden Goblet, The Dragons Den, or any of the
other celebrated taverns -- you will find fighters, thieves, mages, clerics, druids,
rangers, bards, paladins, monks, and barbarians willing to join your cause.

Each companion is unique. There are no duplicates, no respawns. If they fall in
battle, they are gone. Choose your companions wisely, and fight for them.

You can have up to three companions at your side at any time.

## The Bond of Battle

Your companions are not just tag-alongs -- they will die for you.

When you are in serious danger, your companions act:

  At 30% HP: Your most recently recruited companion sacrifices themselves,
              restoring health to keep you in the fight.
  At 15% HP: Another companion steps up.
  At  1% HP: Your last companion gives everything to save your life.

Each fallen companion returns some of their strength to you -- a final gift.

Having companions also grants you small but meaningful bonuses:
more HP, better attacks, better defense, and even bonus experience.

## The Adventurers Guild

A legendary institution has opened its doors.

Visit the guild to:
  - Take on 24 quests -- from escorting merchants to storming ancient tombs.
    Many can be done alone, but they reward a full party.
  - Meet Guildmaster Vorathiel -- a veteran of a hundred campaigns.
  - Buy potions from Sister Marevna, the guild cleric.
  - Purchase magical scrolls from Arcanist Thelmund.
  - Read the Trophy Case -- a testament to the greatest adventuring party ever seen.

And join as many specialty guilds as you dare:

  Fighter Guild   - Combat mastery
  Thief Guild     - Stealth and cunning
  Mage Guild      - Arcane power
  Cleric Guild    - Holy might and healing
  Druid Guild     - Nature's fury

Note: You may only belong to one specialty guild at a time.
Leaving a guild halves your benefits, but your rank is kept.
You can always return and reclaim your full standing.
You may also belong to the Adventurers Guild alongside one specialty guild.

## The Magic of Scrolls

The guild mage sells spell scrolls worn in your ring slots.
Each scroll is charged with a limited number of spells:

  Scroll of Fireball       - Scorching burst of flame           5 charges
  Scroll of Frost Wave     - Freezing wave that slows enemies   6 charges
  Scroll of Lightning Bolt - Crackling bolt of devastation      4 charges
  Scroll of Arcane Shield  - Magical barrier absorbing damage   8 charges
  Scroll of Haste          - Speed and precision in combat      5 charges
  Scroll of Invisibility   - Vanish from sight                  3 charges

When the last charge is spent, the scroll crumbles. Use them when it matters most.

## Guild Recall Scrolls

Each guild sells its own Recall Scroll through their scribe.
These scrolls teleport you directly back to your guild hall.

Warning: Using another guild's scroll without membership will zap you for 10 damage.
The guilds guard their magic jealously.

## The Legend of the Companions

When you are ready, seek out Guildmaster Vorathiel for the greatest quest of your life:

  The Legend of the Companions -- a six-part epic stretching across the land.

  Part 1: The Prophecy Discovered    A buried prophecy stirs something dark.
  Part 2: Gathering the Heroes       Recruit allies for the road ahead.
  Part 3: The Three Seals            Break three ancient seals binding a prison.
  Part 4: The Betrayal at Ashgate    A trusted ally turns traitor.
  Part 5: Siege of the Dark Spire    Storm the tower with your party.
  Part 6: The Final Reckoning        Face the awakened dark lord.

Complete this quest with your party at your side, and your names -- all of them --
will be engraved on the Adventurers Guild trophy case forever.

Solo adventurers are welcome to try. They are not expected to succeed.

## Managing Your Party

  Ask a companion to leave: Talk to them from anywhere in the world.
  Disband your party: Open the party menu and choose Disband All.
  All companions will return to their home pubs.

Companions who die in battle are gone permanently. They gave everything.
Their sacrifice is honoured -- but they cannot be replaced.

The road is long. The nights are dark. But with good companions and sharper steel,
no darkness is permanent.

Now get out there. Your legend is waiting.
