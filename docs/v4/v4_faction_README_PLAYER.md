# ⚔️ JASIAS QUEST: THE FACTION WARS ⚔️
## An Epic New Expansion — Are You Ready to Choose Your Alliances?

---

### THE WORLD IS AT WAR

The lands of Jasias Quest have never been more dangerous — or more alive with opportunity.
Fourteen great races clash across the realm, their ancient grudges and shifting alliances
reshaping the very map of the world. You, brave Wood Elf, stand at the center of it all.

**Will you forge peace? Dominate the battlefield? Or play every side against the middle?
The Faction Wars begin NOW.**

---

### 🗺️ WHAT'S NEW IN THIS EXPANSION

#### 🏰 FOURTEEN FACTIONS — EACH WITH THEIR OWN AGENDA

From the noble Humans of the eastern plains to the terrifying Dragon clans of the volcanic
peaks, every major race now has a living, breathing faction with their own:

- **Stronghold** — A fully explorable headquarters filled with that race's people
- **Faction Leader** — A powerful NPC offering 12 unique quests that shape your relationships
- **Elder Sage** — A mysterious figure who can alter your reputation... for the right price
- **100 unique warriors** — Named individuals who patrol the world, fight for their people,
  and remember if you've crossed them

**The 14 Factions:**
Human • Half-Elf • Elf • Wood Elf • Drow Elf • Sea Elf • Dwarf • Goblin •
Hobgoblin • Gnome • Orc • Ogre • Halfling • Dragon

---

#### 🎯 THE QUEST WEB — 168 FACTION QUESTS

Each faction leader offers 12 quests. Some will build your standing with their people.
Others... might require you to undermine a rival faction. Every choice matters.

Here's the catch: **if you do every single quest, the scales balance perfectly.**
The Faction Wars expansion is designed so that a neutral path IS possible — but playing
favorites will earn you powerful allies and dangerous enemies.

---

#### ⚔️ THE GRAND TOURNAMENT

Travel to the Faction Battlefield and face the champion of each race in single combat.
A true round-robin — 13 fights, one chance per champion. Defeat them all to claim:

**🏆 THE CHAMPION'S MEDALLION OF PEACE** — A legendary neck piece that passively improves
your standing with ALL factions simultaneously. Wear it proudly.

---

#### 🎭 THE ART OF INVISIBILITY

Not every problem requires a sword. The expansion introduces powerful stealth abilities
to bypass hostile factions:

- **Thieves** can learn **Hide in Shadow** — a toggle ability that makes you invisible to
  hostile faction NPCs. It cancels the moment combat starts, so choose your moment wisely.
  *Requires Thieves Guild membership and the Cloak of Shadows.*

- **Mages, Clerics, and Druids** can learn **Invisibility spells** — short-duration spells
  that render you undetectable. *Each guild has its own version with unique duration and cost.*

- Anyone can equip the legendary **Cloak of Invisibility** — a rare item that provides
  permanent passive faction stealth as long as it's worn.

---

#### 🎉 FOUR SEASONAL FESTIVALS — YEAR-ROUND ADVENTURE

The world doesn't stop for the Faction Wars. Seasonal events transform the Festival Grounds
starting **one week before** each holiday and lasting **one week after**:

**🐣 EASTER — Festival of Spring**
The Festival Grounds burst with color. Hunt enchanted eggs, protect spring chicks from foxes,
and face the mysterious Easter Boss for the golden reward: the **Golden Egg of Fortune**.

**🎆 FOURTH OF JULY — Festival of Freedom**
Liberty rings across the realm! Light the freedom beacons, defend the parade, and battle
the Festival Saboteur in an explosive grand finale. Earn the legendary **Freedom Medallion**.

**🎃 HALLOWEEN — Night of Shadows**
The Shadow Witch beckons... The dead walk, pumpkins glow with malevolent light, and
the Pumpkin King awaits those brave enough to challenge him. Claim the **Jack-o-Lantern Helm**
and face what lurks in the darkness.

**🎄 CHRISTMAS — Festival of Winter Lights**
The Winter Sage brings warmth to even the coldest hearts. Deliver gifts, rescue magical
reindeer, and help light the great winter tree. The **Winter Star Amulet** awaits those
who complete the grand celebration.

Each festival features **8 special quests** — one per day of the event, building to a
massive holiday quest with extraordinary rewards. Don't miss a day!

### 🎮 HOW TO INSTALL

1. Copy all files from `res/` directly into your Jasias Quest installation's `res/` folder
2. The `res/values/loadresources.xml` and `res/values/strings.xml` entries should be **merged**
   into your existing files (see the Developer README for details)
3. Launch the game and explore the new Faction Lands area on the world map!
