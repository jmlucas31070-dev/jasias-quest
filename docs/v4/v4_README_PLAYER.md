# Jasias Quest — The Wilds Awakening

## A New Chapter Begins

The world of Jasias has always held secrets — but none so vast, so dangerous, and so rewarding as what now stirs beyond the city gates. The Wilds Awakening is here.

## Meet the Trainers

Across every road, at every crossroads, on the edges of every village — 270 seasoned Trainers now patrol the world. Each one has honed their companions for battle, and they will not let you pass unchallenged. Some will taunt you. Some will respect you. All of them want a fight.

Think you're ready? Face them. Beat them. Earn the glory.

## 375 Creatures Across 15 Regions

The world breathes with life. From the sunlit grasslands to the crushing depths of the ocean, 375 unique creatures now roam the land. Each region has its own ecosystem, its own dangers, and its own treasures:

- **Grassland & Shrubland** — Fast predators and swift prey.
- **Forest & Deep Forest** — Ancient beasts and things that lurk in shadow.
- **Rain Forest** — Exotic, venomous, and breathtakingly dangerous.
- **Desert & Tundra** — Where only the toughest survive the elements.
- **Swamp & Marsh** — Slow death, thick mud, and things below the surface.
- **Mountain & Hills** — High altitude, thin air, and predators that own the sky.
- **Beach, River, Sea & Ocean** — The world does not end at the shoreline.

Every region drops its own unique forage items — gather resources, craft, trade, or hold them as trophies of your travels.

## Three Guilds. One World.

The Mage Guild, the Cleric Order, and the Druid Circle have opened their crafting halls. Join a guild, master your craft:

- **Mages** inscribe powerful arcane scrolls at the writing desk.
- **Clerics** brew sacred potions at the cauldron, blessed by divine grace.
- **Druids** weave nature's power into both scroll and brew.

Each guild has 50 unique abilities — offensive spells to devastate your foes, defensive blessings to shield your allies. Only guild members can use their guild's craft. Carry a portable Writing Table or Mini Cauldron and craft anywhere in the wild.

## Locked Doors & Hidden Chambers

Not every treasure is left in plain sight. Locked doors now guard secret chambers throughout the world.

- **Thieves** can pick locks — carry the right lockpick and try your luck. A Crude Lockpick might work. A Masterwork one almost certainly will.
- **Fighters** can bash doors open — the heavier your shield, the harder you hit.
- Choose the wrong approach and the door will hold. Choose right and riches await.

## The Adventure That Awaits

This is not a small update. This is a world expansion. New creatures. New trainers. New guilds. New ways to solve problems. New things to find around every corner.

Jasias Quest is bigger, richer, and more dangerous than ever before.

**Are you ready? The Wilds are waiting.**
