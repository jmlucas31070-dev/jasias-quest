# ✨ THE NINE TOWERS ✨
## A Legendary Quest Expansion for Jasias Quest

---

### AN ANCIENT MYSTERY REAWAKENED

Nine towers have stood across these lands since before memory. For centuries, they were dormant.
Now they glow with arcane light — and the mages inside them are hostile to all who enter.

Something has claimed them. Something has filled each tower with guardians and sealed a great
magical crystal at the summit of each. Nine towers. Nine crystals. Nine challenges.

**What happens when all nine are united?** That is the question that has kept the sages up at night.
You, Wood Elf, may be the one to answer it.

---

### 🏰 THE NINE TOWERS

Each tower is a multi-floor dungeon of escalating danger, culminating in a powerful Grand Archmage
at the summit who guards a unique colored crystal:

| Tower           | Crystal          | Challenge Level |
|-----------------|------------------|----------------|
| Ruby Tower      | Red Crystal      | ★☆☆☆☆          |
| Amber Tower     | Orange Crystal   | ★★☆☆☆          |
| Solar Tower     | Yellow Crystal   | ★★☆☆☆          |
| Emerald Tower   | Green Crystal    | ★★★☆☆          |
| Sapphire Tower  | Blue Crystal     | ★★★☆☆          |
| Mystic Tower    | Indigo Crystal   | ★★★★☆          |
| Amethyst Tower  | Violet Crystal   | ★★★★☆          |
| Ivory Tower     | White Crystal    | ★★★★★          |
| Obsidian Tower  | Black Crystal    | ★★★★★          |

Each tower has **4 floors of hostile mages** before you reach the top. The mages grow fiercer
with every step upward — and with every tower beyond the first.

Start with the Ruby Tower. Work your way to the Obsidian Tower. Do NOT attempt the Obsidian
Tower first. You have been warned.

---

### 🧙 OUTSIDE QUEST GIVERS

Before each tower stands a mage — a former scholar, adventurer, or scholar who has been
unable to reclaim the tower themselves. They will introduce you to the quest and offer
a unique **side quest** for extra rewards:

- **Aldric Ashbane** waits outside the Ruby Tower, seeking a stolen fire grimoire
- **Faeyra Frostwhisper** near the Amber Tower needs amber dust from cave creatures
- **Gwynar Grimspell** at the Solar Tower has lost three shards of a focusing lens
- **Celindra Coldweave** by the Emerald Tower wants moonleaf herbs from the forest glade
- **Brennus Blazemark** outside the Sapphire Tower needs a water elemental banished
- **Hexara Hexbound** near the Mystic Tower wants void rifts closed
- **Sylvan Stormcaller** at the Amethyst Tower... has lost his shadow cat. Her name is Pixel.
- **Thyra Thornweave** outside the Ivory Tower needs a blessing ritual completed
- **Dravos Duskmantle** by the Obsidian Tower seeks a forbidden tome from the graveyard

Complete the side quests for extra EXP and gold. They're worth it.

---

### 💬 WORD ON THE WIND

Keep an eye out for **24 townspeople** scattered across the world who have heard rumors
of the towers. They can point you in the right direction, hype you up, or share gossip
about the crystals. Some may give you the quest itself if you haven't started it yet.

---

### 🌟 THE TOWER OF CONVERGENCE

There is a tenth tower. You cannot miss it — it appeared recently, and it glows with
every color at once.

Inside, you will find **friendly mages** on every floor, happy to share wisdom:

> *"Magic teaches humility. Explosions teach it faster."*
> *"I once turned myself into a sock. Longest Tuesday of my life."*
> *"The crystals hum different notes. Together, they sing something... enormous."*

At the very top of the Tower of Convergence is the **Crystal Altar**. Place all nine
crystals in it and witness what ancient magic has been waiting an age to reveal.

Your reward: **The Prism of Unity** — a legendary neckpiece that shimmers with all nine
colors of the arcane, granting bonuses to all stats, all faction standings, and experience
gain. There is only one in the world. It will belong to you.

---

### ⚔️ TIPS FOR THE JOURNEY

1. **Start small.** The Ruby Tower is designed for entry-level adventurers. Don't rush to the Obsidian Tower.
2. **Stock healing items** before each floor. The mage damage scales up quickly.
3. **Talk to the outside mages first** — they set the scene and may have side quests that reward you before you even enter.
4. **Don't skip the Convergence Tower's friendly mages.** They say wild things. It's worth it.
5. **The crystals are quest items** — they cannot be sold or dropped, so collect freely.
